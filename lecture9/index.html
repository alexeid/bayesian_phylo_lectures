The Phylogenetic Likelihood

$$P(A|T, Q)$$
  • $A$ is a multiple sequence alignment of $n$ sequences,
  • $T$ is a phylogenetic tree with $n$ leaves, and
  • $Q$ is an instantaneous transition rate matrix parameterizing the CTMC-based model of sequence evolution.

Need a probability distribution for $T$!

The Phylogenetic Posterior

$$P(T,Q|A) = \frac{1}{P(A)}P(A|T,Q)P(T)P(Q)$$
$$P(T,Q|A,M) = \frac{1}{P(A|M)}P(A|T,Q,M)P(T|M)P(Q|M)$$
