\scriptsize{
Proposal scheme: 

\begin{itemize}
\item random direction
\item gamma-distributed step length (mean 45 pixels, s.d. 40 pixels)
\item reflection at edges
\end{itemize}

Target distribution:

\begin{itemize}
\item equal mixture of 3 bivariate normal “hills”
\item inner contours: $50\%$
\item outer contours: $95\%$
\end{itemize}

In this case the robot is accepting every step and 5000 steps are shown}
